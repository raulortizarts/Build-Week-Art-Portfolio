Artify is a freelance collaboration between four part time Lambda Full Stack Developer students. The aim in creating the application was to create a portfolio website that can provide a more professional platform for users work. Inspiration from the site came from Instagram, Pintrest, Dribbble and Behance. Such sites allow fellow creatives a place to share and sell their work.
